Featuring a shared lounge and free WiFi, Victoria Mansion is set in Manila, 2.1 km from Cultural Centre of the Philippines and 2.8 km from Rizal Park. The property is around 2.8 km from Intramuros, 2.9 km from San Agustin Church and 2.9 km from Fort Santiago. The accommodation offers a concierge service, and organising tours for guests.

World Trade Centre Metro Manila is 3.1 km from the guest house, while Manila Cathedral is 3.8 km away. The nearest airport is Manila Ninoy Aquino International Airport, 9 km from Victoria Mansion.
